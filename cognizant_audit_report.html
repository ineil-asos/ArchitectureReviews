Strategic Approach & Methodology

To ensure strategic alignment and maximize return on existing enterprise investments, a rigorous four-phase technical assessment methodology was applied to the initial backlog. Rather than defaulting to custom AI engineering, an "Out-Of-The-Box (OOB) First" philosophy was enforced. Only workflows exhibiting high cognitive complexity, cross-domain orchestration, and unstructured data reasoning were qualified for custom Agentic AI investment.
