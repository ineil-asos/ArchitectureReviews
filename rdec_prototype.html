1. The Agent Roster

Enterprise data is highly siloed. To solve this, specialized Child Agents are deployed directly over each System of Record (ADO, Jira, Confluence). They act as localized expert extractors.

2. Synchronized Crawling

The Child Agents patrol their domains autonomously. Using semantic analysis, they scan hundreds of routine tasks to identify the hidden 'golden' rows indicating technological uncertainty.

3. Orchestrator Synthesis

The Child Agents physically extract the golden facts and carry them back to the Central Orchestrator (The Frontman). The Orchestrator ingests the fragmented data and synthesizes multiple HMRC narratives simultaneously.

4. Autonomous Direct Routing

The Orchestrator seamlessly pushes the drafted narrative cards (Both Tech and Non-Tech) directly into 1-on-1 MS Teams Direct Messages with the specific Engineer or Product Owner for a 1-click confirmation.

5. Human-in-the-Loop Gateway

The ASOS Internal Finance Team acts as a critical governance layer. They review the mathematically guaranteed provenance within the Assurance Hub before formally authorizing the transmission of the Evidence Pack to EY (External Auditors).
